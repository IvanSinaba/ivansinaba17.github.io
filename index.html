a b o u t

One of the areas in Bali which is famous for its alluring tourist attractions is the Ubud area. Apart from the beach, this sub-district has one tourist destination which is quite popular among tourists, namely the Campuhan Ridge Walk. Campuhan Ridge Walk is a beautiful hiking trail that is also used by tourists as a jogging track. Not only the beautiful scenery, Campuhan Ridge Walk also has several other uniqueness and features. Campuhan Ridge Walk is a jogging track around the hills of Campuhan, Bali. This place is located on Jalan Raya Campuhan, Sayan, Ubud, Kelusa, Payangan, Gianyar Regency. The width of this track is only 1.5 meters wide with a length of about 2 kilometers. For those of you who like jogging or leisurely walks, you will definitely like this location.
